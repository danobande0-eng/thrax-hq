Operational Architecture & Protocol FAQ

Review the structural frameworks, legal standards, and field methodologies behind our corporate screening workflows. We approach asset protection and background verification with absolute precision, ensuring complete compliance and institutional data security.

[01] Operational Scope & Field Methodology

What separates THRAX from standard automated background check software?

Standard screening software relies strictly on outdated digital databases that miss critical local nuances. THRAX merges advanced digital OSINT data indexing with on-the-ground physical intelligence verification. Our localized field operatives physically locate declared addresses, perform face-to-face guarantor interviews, and map precise GPS coordinates to completely eliminate fictitious profiles and "ghost addresses."

How do your localized field teams verify candidate addresses?

Our verification process involves physical deployment to the stated address grid. Operatives cross-verify the location with community landmarks, local leadership, or building management, taking down exact spatial coordinates. This multi-layered approach ensures your organization is protected against fraudulent identity claims and insecure residential data points.

[02] Legal Compliance & Institutional Data Security

Is the THRAX screening framework compliant with regional data protection standards?

Absolute asset security and compliance are central to our model. Our screening protocols are fully aligned with prevailing data protection frameworks, including the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC) standards. We enforce strict data classification and clear consent matrices before any candidate assessment or physical tracking is initialized.

How is client and candidate data segregated and encrypted?

All data received through our intake terminal is securely sandboxed and heavily encrypted. We maintain zero open-access databases for corporate briefs, meaning candidate data collected during a Field Intelligence Audit is segregated strictly for your organization's specific review file, protecting you against third-party data leak vulnerabilities.

[03] Engagement Logistics & Turnaround Tiers

What is the standard turnaround time for a complete Background Check Score (BCS) report?

Turnaround timelines are optimized based on the selected urgency tier. Routine preventative assessments (Tier 3) are typically finalized within 48 to 72 operational hours. High-risk financial profiles (Tier 2) or active asset protection concerns (Tier 1) are expedited immediately to minimize corporate risk exposure.

How does the Free Tier-3 Premium Field Intelligence Audit work?

The Free Tier-3 Audit serves as our operational proof-of-concept. Corporate clients can submit the profile metrics of a single candidate completely risk-free. Our team processes the file, deploys ground verification assets, generates a comprehensive baseline report, and delivers it straight to your official inbox to showcase our clinical speed and precision before formal institutional alignment.
